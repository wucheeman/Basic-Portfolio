About Me

I'm a website builder with a twin passion for software development and customer satisfaction. I have over 30 years' experience, fulfilling roles ranging from developer, analyst, and planner to project lead and customer success manager. My capability to apply leading tools such as Bootstrap, Javascript, Node, and MongoDB mean that I can construct powerful, attractive websites for a company or non-profit organization. Moreover, my talents for organization, listening, and clear communication enable me to lead teams delivering million-dollar projects. I am looking forward to using all my skills to deliver new or updated websites for my customers on time and on cost.

I am a graduate of the University of North Carolina's Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp, and I am a certified Project Management Professional (PMP). I hold a Master's degree in Public Affairs from Princeton University as well as a Bacherlor of Art degree in History from the University of Georgia.
